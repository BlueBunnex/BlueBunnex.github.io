my fursona

general

  • A male (he/him) red-yellow plush drake (aka wingless dragon) with a chonky tail who is a little fat.
  • His tail is connected by velcro and is thus detachable.
  • Constantly sleepy, but will always listen if you talk to him.

likes wearing

  • Knee-socks.
  • Aprons.
  • Victorian era bonnets.
  • Sew-on patches.

lore

  • Works part-time at Café de Andréa. His favourite drink is the iced latté, and his favourite menu item is the key-lime pie.
  • Wasn't always plush (the backstory for this is TBD).
