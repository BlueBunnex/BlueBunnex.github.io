I'm taking a creative writing class, which means I've been doing some free-writing. The thing is, I sometimes come across a situation where I don't know a good word to use for a certain situation. However, sort of in the form of an ideophone, a "fake word" pops into my head that I feel sounds fit in context, and so that's what this page is for. Fake words!

polf

/pʌlf/
adjective

1. pillowy (like a bread dough)

“in the mirror she adjusted the polf cap upon her head”

stammy

/’stæ.mi/
adjective

1. (of an open wound) dry but sticky; tacky

“she peeled the bandage from the stammy cut”
