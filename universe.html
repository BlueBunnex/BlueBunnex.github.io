Holly

Favourite food(s): Fried alligator, apples

Of the Duchy of Mondrieg, a sprawling territory reputed for its robust commerce-oriented urban centers and quiet rural landscapes peppered with cottages and cattle, Holly (a member of the its merchant association) engrosses herself in picking apples from her hometown's vast orchard, unaware that not one other resident of Mondrieg actually eats fruit.
