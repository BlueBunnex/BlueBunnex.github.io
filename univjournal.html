assignment

Write a letter or otherwise creatively express how the first 7 weeks of college have been for you. Be sure to include the following information:

Classes
Instructors
Your living situation
New things you have seen or experienced
Balancing the competing demands in your life (i.e. family, school)
Potential majors/careers

This is the second of three journals you will submit. The purpose of these assignments is to provide a forum for you to express your thoughts, feelings, and opinions about topics discussed in class and reflect upon your experiences as you adjust to life at the University of Maryland. Journals are short responses to the assigned question or topic. I welcome creativity in these assignments and hope to see your personality, interests, and strengths shine. You may type your journal or use alternate formats (handwritten journals, video recordings, artistic representations, other media, etc.).
